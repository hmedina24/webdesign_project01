How to make a Steak and Cheese Sub

There are six steps to make Steak and Cheese Sub


Step 1: Butter hoagie rolls, dice onion and thinly sliced steak

First we slice hoagie rolls 3/4 of the way through with
a serrated knife. Dice onions and thinly slice beef.

Step 2:Sautee onions and remove

The second step stir together 2 Tbsp softened butter
with 1 pressed garlic clove. Spread garlic butter onto the cut sides of
4 hoagie roll. Toast the buns on a large skillet, flat cooktop or griddle
on medium heat until golden brown then set aside.

Step 3: Transfer to a bowl

The third step Add 1 Tbsp oil to your pan/cooktop
and sautee diced onions until caramelized then transfer to a bowl.

Step 4: Sautee beef until cooked through and add back onions

The fourth step is Increase to high heat and add 1 Tbsp
oil. Spread the super thinly sliced steak in an even layer. Let brown for
a couple of minutes undisturbed then flip and season with 1/2 tsp salt and
1/2 tsp black pepper. Sautee until steak is fully cooked through then stir
in the caramelized onions.

Step 5: Divide into 4 portions, top each with 2 slices cheese

The second to last step Divide into 4 even portions
and top each with 2 slices of cheese and turn off the heat so the cheese
will melt without overcooking the meat.

Step 6: Cover with buns and scrape into buns with a spatula

Last but not least spread a thin layer of mayo on the
toasted side of each roll. Working with one portion at a time, place a
toasted bun over each portion and use a spatula to scrape the cheesy beef
into your bun as you flip it over. Serve warm.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ribeye steak, trimmed and thinly sliced
  • 1/2 tsp Sea salt, or to taste
  • 1/2 tsp Black pepper, or to taste
  • 1 sweet onion, (large), diced
  • 8 slices provolone cheese, mild (not aged provolone)
  • 4 Hoagie Rolls, sliced 3/4 through
  • 2 Tbsp unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 garlic clove, pressed
  • 2-4 Tbsp mayonnaise, or to taste

Makes 1 serving
